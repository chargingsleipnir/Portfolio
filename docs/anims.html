Eerie Fire Truck



About the Fire Truck:

This was a midterm project using Blender. Building the models was great, but I was shocked to find how much I enjoyed animating! I was so happy to spend every waking hour setting keyframes, manipulating f-curves, and doing as much with my 60 seconds as possible. Try not to quake in your boots!
